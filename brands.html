Key Product Terms

The following description list explains common product technologies you’ll see:

Foam midsole
Cushioning underfoot that can vary in firmness and energy return.
Primeknit / Flyknit
Engineered knit uppers that improve breathability and fit.
Carbon plate
A stiff plate embedded in some racing shoes to aid propulsion.
