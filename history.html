Milestones in Sportswear

From track spikes to super-shoes, sports brands evolved through material science and athlete feedback.

Timeline

  1. 1949 – Adidas is founded; screw-in studs popularize adaptable traction.
  2. 1964 – Blue Ribbon Sports (later Nike) starts distributing performance shoes.
  3. 1970s – Air cushioning and EVA foams enter mainstream footwear.
  4. 1990s – Moisture-wicking synthetics and compression apparel grow.
  5. 2010s – Knit uppers and energy-return foams redefine marathon racing.

Why Materials Matter

Lightweight uppers reduce fatigue, responsive foams improve efficiency, and durable outsoles extend product life.
